Our Calling

Awakened by the Word,
Sent by the Spirit.

Young Revivalist Academy exists for one purpose: to form men and women who know the Scriptures deeply and carry them boldly. Our classrooms sit beside our chapel for a reason — here, doctrine and devotion are never separated.

Every student walks a path built on the whole counsel of Scripture, mentored community, and real ministry practice long before graduation day.

Four Years, One Calling

The Path to Ministry

  1. 01

    Foundation Year

    Old and New Testament survey, an introduction to biblical Greek and Hebrew, and the spiritual disciplines that will carry a student through the years ahead.

  2. 02

    Discipleship Track

    Hermeneutics and systematic theology, paired with a mentored ministry placement in a local church.

  3. 03

    Ministry Specialization

    Students choose a concentration — Pastoral Ministry, Worship, Missions, or Biblical Counseling — and go deep.

  4. 04

    Commissioning

    A capstone ministry project, ordination preparation, and a sending service before graduates go where they're called.

Choose Your Track

Four Tracks. One Foundation.

Track I

Pastoral Ministry

Preaching, church leadership, and the shepherding of a congregation through every season of its life.

Track II

Worship & Music

Theology of worship, musicianship, and leading a congregation into the presence of God with excellence.

Track III

Missions & Evangelism

Cross-cultural ministry, church planting, and the practical craft of taking the gospel to unreached places.

Track IV

Biblical Counseling

Caring for the wounded with Scripture, sound theology, and a shepherd's patience.
